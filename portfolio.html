About me.

Hi, I'm

It all started in a small village in West Bengal, where I first discovered editing and design. I began learning on a phone, then moved to a normal PC that lagged almost every time I opened Premiere Pro or After Effects — but I kept learning anyway.

Coming from a middle-class family, buying expensive courses or setups was never an option. Everything I know today was learned through free resources, countless late nights, and constant practice.

Over time, that passion turned into my profession. Since November 2025, I've been working at YAAS Media as a video editor and motion designer, creating short-form and long-form content while continuing to grow every day.

For me, editing is more than just work — it's proof that consistency can take you further than circumstances.
